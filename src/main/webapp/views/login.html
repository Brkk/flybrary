What is the Flybrary?

A Sharing network for students.

The flybrary connects students to share textbooks. Add a book and we do the searching for you to match you up.

How does it work?

Easily make a request for a textbook that you want. Done with a textbook? Offer a textbook so that you can share it with a friend you haven’t met yet. How do you trade? You decide, discuss it with your match through email. Don’t worry, all your information is private.
